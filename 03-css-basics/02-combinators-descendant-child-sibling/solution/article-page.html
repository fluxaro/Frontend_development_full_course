What Are Combinators?

Combinators define the relationship between selectors. They let you target elements based on their position in the HTML tree.

There are four combinators in CSS: descendant, child, adjacent sibling, and general sibling.

"CSS combinators are one of the most powerful tools for writing clean, class-free CSS."

The Child Combinator
