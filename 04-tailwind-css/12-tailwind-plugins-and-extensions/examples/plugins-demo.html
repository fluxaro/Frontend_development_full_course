Prose Size Variants

prose-sm

Small prose — good for compact content areas, sidebars, and footnotes.

prose (base)

Base prose — the default size, good for most article content.

prose-lg

Large prose — great for featured articles and long-form reading.

prose-xl

Extra large prose — hero articles and editorial layouts.

prose-invert — Dark Background

class="prose prose-invert prose-lg"

Dark Mode Article

The prose-invert modifier automatically switches all prose colors to work beautifully on dark backgrounds. No extra configuration needed.

  • Text becomes light
  • Links stay visible
  • Code blocks adapt
prose-invert is the easiest way to support dark mode in article content.
